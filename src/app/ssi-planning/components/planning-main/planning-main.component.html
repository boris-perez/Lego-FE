Security plans

The Processes of Administration of Industrial Security belong to the Sub-System of Maintenance of Human Resources, which can be mentioned:

  • Accident prevention.
  • Fire protection.
  • Environmental Protection.
  • Labor Ergonomics.
  • Occupational medicine.
  • Industrial Security Administration.

These processes remain closely interrelated and interdependent. Their interaction requires that any change in one of them, influence the others, which will cause new changes in the others and will generate adaptations and adjustments throughout the system
